ANSWERS TO FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can you accept international payments?

Yes, we accept international payments. You can use major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods to join our program, regardless of your location.

CONTRAINDICATIONS for participation:

Participation in the program may not be suitable for individuals with certain health conditions. If you have any chronic diseases, recent injuries, or other medical concerns, please consult with your healthcare provider before joining. It's essential to ensure that the exercises are safe for your specific health situation.

When does the program start/end?

The program is designed to start whenever you are ready. You will have access to all the materials and instructions upon registration. The duration of the program is typically 12 weeks, but you can pace yourself according to your personal schedule.

I work full time, and also have a baby, do housecleaning, cooking, etc. What should I do?

We understand that life can be very busy. Our program is flexible and can be adjusted to fit your schedule. You can do the exercises at any time that is convenient for you, whether it's early in the morning, during your lunch break, or in the evening after your household duties.

Do I need to purchase equipment to exercise with you?

No, you do not need to purchase any special equipment to participate in our program. All exercises are designed to be done using your body weight and common household items.

How many times a week should I do the exercises?

For optimal results, we recommend doing the exercises 3-4 times a week. However, the program is flexible, and you can adjust the frequency based on your personal schedule and fitness level.

I can't do the exercises every day. What should I do?

That's perfectly fine. The program is designed to be flexible. You can follow the exercises at your own pace and adjust the frequency to fit your schedule. Consistency is more important than daily exercise.

How do I receive tasks and where should I send the reports?

You will receive your tasks and exercises through our online platform. Detailed instructions and videos will be available in your account. Reports can be submitted directly through the platform or sent via email, depending on what is more convenient for you.

What's the deadline for sending a daily report?

There is no strict deadline for sending daily reports. However, for the best results and to track your progress effectively, it is recommended to submit your report by the end of each day after completing the exercises.

Is it mandatory to send the report?

Submitting reports is not mandatory but highly encouraged. Sending reports helps you stay accountable and allows us to provide you with personalized feedback and support.

Is my health suitable for the program?

If you are generally healthy and have no major medical conditions, you should be able to participate in the program. However, if you have any health concerns, it is important to consult with your healthcare provider before starting the program to ensure it is safe for you.

Can I participate if I have a chronic disease?

Participation may be possible if you have a chronic disease, but it is crucial to consult with your healthcare provider first. They can provide guidance on whether the exercises are appropriate for your condition and any modifications you might need.

Are there weight/age limitations?

There are no specific weight or age limitations for participating in our program. The exercises can be modified to suit various fitness levels and ages. However, we recommend consulting with a healthcare provider if you have any specific concerns.

Can I participate in the program if I live in another country?

Yes, you can participate in the program from anywhere in the world. Our online platform allows you to access all materials and support remotely.

How can I pay for my participation in the project if I am abroad?

International participants can pay using major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods. We strive to make the payment process as smooth as possible for participants from all over the world.

I have paid for my participation. When do I receive my login name and password?

Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive an email with your login name and password within 24 hours. This will grant you access to our online platform where you can start the program immediately. If you do not receive your login details within this timeframe, please contact our support team for assistance.
