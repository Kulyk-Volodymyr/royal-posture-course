✖ THE PROGRAM IS NOT SUITABLE FOR:

Pregnant

Safety of the fetus is the first priority during pregnancy. Any physical exercises must be approved by your doctor.

✖ Those who want to lose weight - This is not a weight loss marathon. But! Participants can reduce weight by 3-5kg by activating their lymphatic system and getting rid of excess fluid. The waist reduction can be from 5cm to 15cm.
